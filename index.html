Kung Pao Chicken 宫保鸡丁

Introduction

This is a homemade dish in Sichuan cuisine. It is popluar in China because of the fresh chicken with peanuts crisp.

这是一道川菜中的保留家常菜.由于其入口鲜辣，鸡肉的鲜嫩配合花生的香脆，深受大众喜欢。

Ingredient

Chicken meat ingredients 250g; 200g peanuts; accessories salt ; chili onion; pepper ; soy sauce, garlic; sugar

主料鸡脯肉250g,花生米200g,辅料葱适量,盐适量,干辣椒适量,花椒适量,蒜适量,酱油适量,醋适量,料洒适量,鲜汤适量,姜适量,糖适量,红薯淀粉适量

Method

1.Slices of ginger and garlic. 姜、蒜去皮切片

2. Onion diced. 葱白切成颗粒状

3. Chilli chilli cut into 1 centimeters long. 干辣椒去蒂去籽，切成1厘米长的节

4.Cut Chicken into little pice.鸡肉拍松，再用刀改成小丁

5.Mix with salt, soy sauce, rice wine and sweet potato starch. 用盐、酱油、料酒红薯淀粉拌匀上浆去腥

6. Put peanuts in an oil pan and fry crisp. 6.花生米洗净，放入油锅炸脆

7. out of cooling. 捞出冷却

8.put oil,Ginger, garlic and Sichuan Pepper烧热锅，下油,花椒,姜，蒜

9.Then put fried chicken.随即下入炒变色的鸡丁炒散

10.Pour the fried peanuts in the pot and stir fry the juices and put them in the plate.起锅前倒入炸好的花生米，翻炒收汁起锅装盘
